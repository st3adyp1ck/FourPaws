Paw-some Tips and Tricks

Got a furry friend that thinks bath time is a full-contact sport? Or a cat that believes furniture is their personal scratching post? We’ve got your back! Dive into our collection of pet hacks, pro grooming tips, and training tricks to make life with your furball (mostly) mess-free and a lot more fun!
"The Treat Trap"    
Tip: Have a pet that won’t sit still for grooming? Try the ol’ peanut butter on the wall trick! Smear a little on a smooth surface, and watch your dog stay put while you work your magic.

• Why It Works: They’re too busy enjoying the treat to notice they’re getting their nails trimmed!

“Cat Scratching Conundrum”    
Tip: Tired of your cat treating your couch like a personal scratching post? Sprinkle a little catnip on their designated scratcher and move it close to their favorite spot. They’ll get the hint... eventually!

• Why It Works: Cats love catnip, and they’ll soon realize their new scratcher is the place to be.
“Hide & Treat Seek”
Tip: Need to keep your dog entertained? Hide treats around the house and let them go on a scavenger hunt. It’s like treasure hunting for pups!

• Why It Works: It keeps their nose and mind busy, plus it’s great exercise (for both them and you when they find that treat you forgot about)

“Fishy Water Woes”
Tip: Does your cat refuse to drink from their water bowl? Try a water fountain! Cats love running water (it’s like their own mini waterfall), and it’ll have them staying hydrated in no time.

• Why It Works: The movement catches their attention, making it more interesting than plain ol' standing water.
“Bed Swap Magic”
Tip: Your dog ignoring that fancy bed you bought? Place one of your old t-shirts in it—the smell of you will make it instantly cozy for them.

• Why It Works: Dogs love their humans, and this little trick makes the bed feel like a safe, comfy spot. Plus, it’s free!
